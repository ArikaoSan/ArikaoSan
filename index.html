About ArikaoSan

Born in 2004 in Morocco, ArikaoSan is a self-taught pianist playing Classical and J-pop Music. He started playing the piano in July 2021 and now owns a YouTube channel with almost 200 followers (as of February 2025). While he hasn't achieved high results in his career yet, he is always fighting to achieve his dreams. All links to his social media and content are available here. Thank you for visiting!
